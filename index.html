About

Mohammad Mosab Ibrahim known as the rapper Ibrahim Basha NuruleZ. He is a Syrian rapper . He was born and raised in Kuwait. NuruleZ started his journey in his early age in 2005 in Kuwait, then, he moved to Syria in 2008 to continue his passion in being a rapper. Ibrahim Basha NuruleZ is a talented and professional sound engineer, songwriter, rapper and video editor.
